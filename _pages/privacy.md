This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit **Kyung-Min Jin's personal blog** (https://kyungminjin.github.io).

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including:
- **IP address** and general geographic location (city/country level)
- **Browser type and version**
- **Operating system**
- **Referring website**
- **Pages visited and time spent**

This information is collected via:
- **Google Analytics** (traffic analysis, page views, user behavior)
- **Visitor Map widgets** (geographic distribution of visitors)

### Comments
We use **utterances** (GitHub-based comment system) for blog post comments. When you leave a comment:
- Your comment is stored in the GitHub Issues of this site's repository
- Your **GitHub username and profile** will be publicly visible
- We do not collect additional personal information beyond what GitHub provides

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Understand how visitors use our Site
- Improve content and user experience
- Analyze traffic patterns and popular content
- Detect and prevent technical issues
